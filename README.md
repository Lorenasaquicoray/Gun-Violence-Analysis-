## Course
Statistical Computing and Graphics in R

## Project Overview
This project analyzes gun violence trends in the United States using data from the Gun Violence Archive and U.S. Census data. The project explores national and state-level trends, geographic patterns, fatalities, injuries, and incident characteristics to better understand the impact of gun violence on public health.

Using R, we performed data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, visualization, and geographic analysis to identify patterns and trends over time.

## Research Questions
- How have gun violence trends changed over time in the United States?
- How do gun violence trends in Michigan compare to national trends?
- What incident characteristics are most common?
- What geographic patterns can be identified across states and counties?
